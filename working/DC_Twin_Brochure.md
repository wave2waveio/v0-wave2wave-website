Traditional cabling approaches rely on bulk catalog ordering and on-site improvisation. The result: cable chaos, installation delays, and infrastructure that's difficult to document or audit. Your team knows the cost: 30-50% of installation time wasted fixing problems that should never have happened.

Every length is calculated from real routing—not straight-line estimates. Every connection is mapped from source port to destination port.

You approve the design. We manufacture to specification. You install with confidence.

## Print Production Notes

### For Design Team

**Software Recommendations:**
- Adobe InDesign (preferred for print layout)
- Adobe Illustrator (for graphics/icons)
- Adobe Photoshop (for image editing)

**Export Settings:**
- PDF/X-1a:2001 or PDF/X-4 format for printer
- Include bleed and crop marks
- Embed all fonts
- Convert all colors to CMYK

**Proofing:**
- Request print proof before full run
- Verify color accuracy (especially Electric Blue)
- Check image sharpness at 100% scale
- Confirm all text is readable at smallest size

### Print Specifications

**Recommended Paper:**
- **Weight:** 100lb gloss cover stock or 80lb gloss text (premium feel)
- **Finish:** Gloss or semi-gloss (matte acceptable for conservative audiences)
- **Coating:** Aqueous or UV coating for durability

**Quantity Recommendations:**
- Initial print run: 250-500 copies
- Reorder as needed (avoid large runs if content may update)

**Distribution:**
- Sales team leave-behinds (conferences, meetings)
- Trade show handouts (pair with business card)
- Direct mail to qualified prospects
- PDF version for email distribution
