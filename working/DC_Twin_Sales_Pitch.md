* **Issue resolution** — Problems surface at the factory, not in your data center

## Slide 7: Project Workflow

**Headline:** Our Process: From Your Data to Your Loading Dock in 3-4 Weeks

**Subhead:** Six steps. One accountable partner.

**Process Steps:**

**1. Discovery & Data Collection** (2-3 days)
You share your rack layout (CAD/Visio) and configuration data (spreadsheet). We review port requirements, pathway constraints, and project timeline.

**2. Digital Twin Engineering** (5-7 days)
We build your complete 3D model in the DC Twin™ platform. Racks, switches, cable trays, and pathways are modeled. Every cable is routed and calculated.

**3. Documentation & BOM Generation** (2-3 days)
Complete BOM created with all specifications. Installation guides generated showing every connection. Test documentation templates prepared.

**4. Design Review** (3-5 days)
You review the 3D model, cable specifications, BOM, and pricing. We refine as needed until design is approved for manufacturing.

**5. Manufacturing & Testing** (2-3 weeks)
Custom cables manufactured to calculated lengths. Factory-labeled and 100% tested. Organized into rack-specific kits.

**6. Delivery & Support** (1-2 days shipping)
Rack-organized kits ship to your site with complete documentation package. We're available for questions during installation.

**Timeline:** Typical project: 3-4 weeks from design data to delivery (vs. 8-12 week industry average)

**Speaker Notes:**
Walk through the timeline. Emphasize the speed: 3-4 weeks is a competitive advantage. Also emphasize collaboration: they approve before manufacturing (customer control + our accountability).

## Presentation Guidelines

### Delivery Tips for Sales Team

**Slide Pacing:**
- Allocate 2-3 minutes per slide for a 20-25 minute presentation
- Allow extra time on Slide 4 (Deliverables) and Slide 8 (Case Study) for Q&A
- Save 5-10 minutes at end for discussion

**Audience Adaptation:**
- For engineering audiences: Go deeper on Slides 5-6 (technical methodology)
- For procurement audiences: Emphasize Slide 4 (complete package, single PO)
- For executive audiences: Focus on Slide 2 (problem/cost) and Slide 8 (results)

**Imagery Notes:**
- All image placeholders should be replaced with actual project photos and screenshots
- Maintain consistent color scheme: Dark Charcoal Gray (#333333) and Electric Blue (#0072CE)
- Ensure all screenshots are high resolution (min 1200px width) and readable when projected
- Add subtle borders or drop shadows to screenshots for visual separation

**Brand Consistency:**
- Use Wave2Wave.io brand assets (logo, colors, fonts per brand guidelines)
- Maintain professional engineering tone throughout (avoid marketing hyperbole)
- Include ISO 9001/14001 certification badges if space allows

### File Formats & Distribution

**Recommended Export Formats:**
- PowerPoint (.pptx) — Primary format for editing and presenting
- PDF — For email distribution and client review
- Google Slides — For collaborative editing and cloud access

**Asset Preparation:**
- Gather all referenced images from project files before finalizing
- Ensure image licensing allows commercial use
- Compress final file to <25MB for email distribution
